A bit about me

Seth Epstein

Web Designer / Graphic Designer Extraordinaire


I graduated from Emory University in 2004, with a BA in Psychology. In 2005 I was hired by PinDotMedia as a graphic designer. Within 6 months I was promoted to Art Director, and stayed with the agency for 7 years. As a designer, it became evident that within our industry there was an ever increasing trend towards internet based marketing strategies. As a result of which, I decided it was time to "Sink or Swim" and study Web Design in Chicago at The Starter League located at 1871 . Currently I am a developer at Shafer Condon Carter where I work with the following clients: Ad Council, Campbells, Cubs, ConAgra Foods, First Midwest Bank, Fusion Academy, Giordanos, Ideal Industries, Kerrygold, Pork Foodservice, Proctor & Gamble, Rich's Foodservice, Solo, Unlce Dougies, & Terlato Wines.
