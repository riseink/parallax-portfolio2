Account Administrators,

You can now add, edit and delete travelers in your company's online profile at www.AmTrav.com. It's easy. Follow the step-by-step instructions below.

  1. Login to www.AmTrav.com
  2. Click on the orange "Account Administrator" button
  3. Go to the "Users & Travelers" section. From here you can:
  • Add new users using the [Add New] button

  • Send invitations using the [Send Invitation] button

  • Edit/Delete existing traveler profiles

Play the following 3-minute video for an overview on this functionality. In addition, there is a live 15-minute webinar this Friday, September 6th at 1pm CT. RSVP directly to Kyle Morrison (kylem@amtrav.com).

You can also edit the rest of your company's profile online at www.AmTrav.com . This includes but is not limited to:

  • Corporate Credit Cards
  • Rewards Program Information
  • Billing Reference Codes
